# Product Narrative

## What Nestify Is

Nestify is a household command center for the place you live.

It helps renters, homeowners, couples, families, and roommates know what needs attention, keep proof organized, stay ahead of household tasks, and handle issues without carrying all of it in their head.

Nestify is not:

- a generic home tracker
- a random dashboard
- only a utility bill app
- a provider-sync app
- a generic AI chatbot
- a marketplace or payments product

Provider sync is optional automation. Manual workflows are first-class.

## Core Promise

Know what needs attention, keep proof organized, and stay ahead of household tasks without carrying it all in your head.

## Target Users

- Renters
- Homeowners
- Couples
- Families
- Roommates
- Anyone responsible for the place they live

## Core Jobs

1. Know what needs attention.
2. Track what is due.
3. Keep important proof organized.
4. Stay ahead of care tasks and reminders.
5. Handle household issues.
6. Reduce mental load.
7. Create useful household history over time.

## Primary Product Loop

Add something important
-> Nestify organizes it
-> Dashboard tells you what matters now
-> the user handles it
-> activity/history builds trust
-> the user comes back when something is due, changes, breaks, or they need proof

## Aha Moment

Within the first session, the user should understand:

“I can put household bills, records, tasks, and issues here, and Nestify will tell me what needs attention.”

## Activation Event

The user completes at least one of:

- adds a bill with due date
- saves an important document
- adds a care reminder
- starts an issue check and creates a follow-up task

## Return Triggers

- bill due
- task due
- issue follow-up
- document renewal
- something breaks
- user needs a receipt, policy, lease, warranty, manual, or bill PDF

## Page Jobs

- Dashboard: what matters now and what to do next
- Bills: household costs, due dates, and paid status
- Vault: important records and proof
- Care: reminders, recurring tasks, repairs, and follow-ups
- Help: guided household issue intake and safe next steps
- Providers: optional automation sources
- Home: place context that makes the app more useful

## Product Rules

- One primary action per page.
- Dashboard never becomes a cleanup board for invalid data created by forms.
- Empty states teach the product and offer one clear next step.
- No repeated CTA spam.
- No provider setup nags unless tied to real value.
- No fake precision, fake production data, or fake AI claims.
